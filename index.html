Geography (from Greek: γεωγραφία, geographia. [1] Geography is an all-encompassing discipline that seeks an understanding of Earth and its human and natural complexities—not merely where objects are, but also how they have changed and come to be. While geography is specific to Earth, many concepts can be applied more broadly to other celestial bodies in the field of planetary science. [2] One such concept, the first law of geography, proposed by Waldo Tobler, is "everything is related to everything else, but near things are more related than distant things."[3][4] Geography has been called "the world discipline" and "the bridge between the human and the physical sciences."

This project aims to explore the Spotify dataset to gain insights into music trends and preferences using visualizations. By creating visualizations of this data, we can gain a better understanding of the music preferences of Spotify users and identify trends in the music industry. The primary data source for this project is the Spotify dataset, which contains information on songs, artists, and user behaviors on the platform. The dataset includes features such as the trend of songs, the streams, chart as well as information on artist genres and album release dates. I used variety of visualization techniques, including bar charts, line graphs, and heatmaps. I used Python's matplotlib library to create these visualizations. I have also performed data cleaning and preprocessing to ensure that the visualizations accurately reflect the data.
