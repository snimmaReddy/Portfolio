Android Application for Password Management

This android app aids users in creating secure passwords for their own protection in the web. In today's digital world, password management is one of the most underutilized virtual tasks. The capabilities for managing passwords are continually being updated by different businesses. The purpose with the use of a password manager, both people and organizations can store and manage all their passwords in one secure location. Desktop password managers keep your passwords securely stored locally on your device, such as your laptop. These credentials are not accessible from any other device. So, instead of having and maintaining two different apps in your system, we'll be developing a single app which can perform both activities. Our App enables the user to manage their passwords by generating random passwords and use them or store the password created by the users and secure them.

Identification of Fake news using deep learning architecture

Society and individuals are negatively influenced both politically and socially by the widespread increase of fake news either way generated by humans or machines. Therefore, automated fake news detection tools have become a crucial requirement. We used a hybrid Neural Network architecture, that combines the capabilities of CNN and LSTM, is used with two different dimensionality reduction approaches, Principle Component Analysis (PCA) and Chi-Square. This work acquired a dataset from the Fake News Challenges (FNC) website which has four types of stances: agree, disagree, discuss, and unrelated. The nonlinear features are fed to PCA and chi-square which provides more contextual features for fake news detection. The motivation of this research is to determine the relative stance of a news article towards its headline. The experimental results show that PCA outperforms than Chi-square and state-of-the-art methods with 97.8% accuracy.

Home Surveillance and motion detection with Raspberry Pi, Open CV, Dropbox

This project deals with Home Surveillance and motion detection using raspberry pi, python and capture the picture and send it to the drop box. Motion detection is the process of detecting a change in the position of an object relative to its surroundings. The intension of this project is to monitor the house and alert the owner if anything appears out of ordinary and saves a backup copy in the Dropbox account. The proposed Raspberry Pi is connected with a Pi camera and internet connection, the whole device can be configured to carry out the surveillance tasks. This project is to prevent loss of privacy and assets. This smart surveillance monitoring system store the images of intruders and notify the owners through drop box account that triggered the motion.

Spotify Charts Analysis

This project aims to explore the Spotify dataset to gain insights into music trends and preferences using visualizations. By creating visualizations of this data, we can gain a better understanding of the music preferences of Spotify users and identify trends in the music industry. The primary data source for this project is the Spotify dataset, which contains information on songs, artists, and user behaviors on the platform. The dataset includes features such as the trend of songs, the streams, chart as well as information on artist genres and album release dates. I used variety of visualization techniques, including bar charts, line graphs, and heatmaps. I used Python's matplotlib library to create these visualizations. I have also performed data cleaning and preprocessing to ensure that the visualizations accurately reflect the data.
